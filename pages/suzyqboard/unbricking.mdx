---
title: Unbricking
description: Unbrick your ChromeOS Device using the SuzyQ Board
---

When Unbricking, the Fyra SuzyQ Board is no different then using a SuzyQable, so you can just follow follow [MrChromebox's SuzyQ Unbricking page](https://docs.mrchromebox.tech/docs/support/unbricking/unbrick-suzyq.html).

Note that this WILL work in Ultramarine Linux/Fedora, and you may `dnf install` `flashrom` from Fedora repositories, and `cbfstool` from [Terra](https://terra.fyralabs.com/).
